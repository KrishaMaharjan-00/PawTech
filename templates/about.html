About Us

Our Mission

Making a proper network platform to book the petsitters by just one click. As well as giving information about the rescue shelters in the areas. This system also helps to find homeless puppies a home and people can adopt them by contacting the particular shelters of person.

This system is to help those who find themselves in this situation. Paw Tech will give information of most of the active sheltersn like : Shree’s Animal Rescue Nepal, KAT Centre, Together for paws, Sneha’s Care, Animal Nepal, HART, Soltis’ Hope Street Animal Rescue, I Care You, Street Dog Care, Community Dog Welfare Kopan and so on.

What We Offer

Travel in comfort

Enjoy your holiday with added peace of mind knowing your pets are well looked after.

Safe and happy pets

From dog walks to belly rubs, sitters keep your pets happy in the one place they feel truely relaxed like their own home.

A win-win for everyone

With a community of sitters looking to stay with pets like yours, there's no need to ask friends or family for help.
"Every human should not buy a dog but rescue in need and free him from sufferings and give him love, care and shelter."
